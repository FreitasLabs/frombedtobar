Garden Tips

Grow a better bar cart.

  • 01
    Start with herbs
    Mint, basil, rosemary, and thyme are easy to grow, hard to kill, and they'll show up in more recipes than anything else you plant.
  • 02
    Contain your mint
    Mint spreads aggressively. Plant it in its own pot or it will take over everything nearby — ask any gardener who learned the hard way.
  • 03
    Harvest before it flowers
    Herbs get bitter once they bolt. Pick regularly to keep the plant productive and the flavor where you want it.
